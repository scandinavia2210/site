Mining & Quarrying

Exploring Rwanda's Rich Mineral and Quarry Resources

What We Mine & Quarry

At Scandinavia Rwanda Ltd, we are dedicated to responsibly extracting and processing a variety of valuable minerals and materials that are essential for Rwanda's development and industrial growth. Our operations include:

  • Iron Ores: Extraction and processing of iron ore for use in construction, manufacturing, and infrastructure projects.
  • Uranium and Thorium Ores: Mining of uranium and thorium, which are critical for energy production and advanced technologies.
  • Non-Ferrous Metal Ores: Sourcing and refining metals such as copper, aluminum, and other valuable non-ferrous resources.
  • Quarrying of Stone, Sand, and Clay: Supplying high-quality stone, sand, and clay for construction, road building, and other industrial uses.

We are committed to sustainable practices, environmental stewardship, and supporting local communities through our mining and quarrying activities.
